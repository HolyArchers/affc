Welcome to Abundant Faith Fellowship Church!

Pastor and Founder W. Trey Davis

W. Trey Davis is the Senior Pastor and founder of Abundant Faith Fellowship Church. Prior to launching AFFC, he served as Minister of Music at New Covenant Christian Church in Houston, TX for 14 years. Pastor "D" currently holds a Bachelors of Arts Degree in Fine Arts from Texas Southern University, as well as a teaching certificate in General Education from the state of Texas. His future academic plans include attending Houston Graduate School of Theology to pursue his Masters of Divinity. Although Pastor and Lady "D" are passionate about serving the AFFC Family, their greatest joy comes from parenting their two beautiful daughters; Meghan Elyse and Kirstyn Naomi.
